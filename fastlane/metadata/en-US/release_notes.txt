Roshi 2.0 is the Mac App Store release of the local-first workbench for testing LLM APIs.

Highlights:
- Multi-provider support: OpenAI, Anthropic, Google Gemini, OpenRouter, and custom OpenAI-compatible endpoints
- Real-time streaming with raw request/response inspection and copy-as-cURL
- Eval mode to compare prompts across models with metrics and LLM-as-judge scoring
- Collections, environments, request tabs, and a command palette for fast iteration
- Local-first and private: API keys, history, and settings stay on your device — no account, no telemetry

This release also lets you save exports to any location you choose, aligns the content-security policy across web and desktop builds, and includes streaming and API-key privacy hardening.
